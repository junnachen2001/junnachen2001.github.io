about me

Hello! I'm Junna (pronounced June-ah)!


I'm a second year student at the University of California, Berkeley, studying Computer Science. Currently, you can find me quarantining in San Diego, California. Outside of my classes, I also enjoy dancing, ice skating, reading, and lettering (a type of calligraphy).


Fun Fact: all the lettering on this website was created by me!

My Story

Going into college, I was unsure on what I wanted to pursue in the future. There were quite a few fields of study that I was interested in, but I evenutally found that computer science perfectly fit my interests in design, problem solving, and logical thinking.

Since computer science is such a vast field that encompasses so many other sub-topics, I wanted to spend my first few years in college exploring the different paths available for me to take. Apart from my core courses at Berkeley, I have also taken steps to learn more about iOS development, web development, and cloud computing. Along the way, I found myself also drawn to cognitive science and design, both of which mesh perfectly with my original computer science interest.

About This Page

I wanted to create a page that would not only showcase my frontend skills, but also challenge me and help me grow as a programmer. This website is entirely designed and developed by me, and is one of my proudest project to date. If you are curious about anything you see, from the color scheme, to any of my projects, feel free to contact me! I would love to chat!
